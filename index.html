About Me

I recently graduated from Washington University with a Master's degree in Computer Science. In addition to having experience with web and app development through work and internships, I have hands-on service experience through AmeriCorps.

I am passionate about advocating for the underprivileged, and working with a low-income school demonstrated how much change can be made if we prioritize equity and justice. I also learned the importance of working personally with disadvantaged communities to fully understand their situation and needs. My goal is to pair that understanding with my skills in tech and programming to properly help and advocate for those that need it most.

Additionally, I am very proud of the work I've done as co-president of my school's chapter of ACM. Managing the logistics for events, diplomatically seeking solutions to interpersonal problems, and finding the balance between kind and firm has really taught me a lot about working with people and has helped me become the kind of leader I want to be.
